Our Company

Our company started in 1983 when our founder said: "Let there be greenhouses!"

We are Greenhouses, Inc, a family run, employee owned business that is the 4th largest greenhouse manufacturer in the world. We have the most reliable greenhouse designs, making 100's of thousands of greenhouses a year. We are very proud of our excellent greenhouses. Since 1983 we have been committed to quality and we always strive for your satisfaction.
